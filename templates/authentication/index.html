Expense Tracking

Users can input and categorize their expenses, including details such as date, amount, category, and description. This functionality allows users to keep track of where their money is going and analyze spending patterns.

Income Tracking

Budgeting

Users can set financial goals and allocate specific amounts of money to different spending categories or savings targets. The website may provide visual representations, such as charts or graphs, to help users visualize their progress towards their budgeting goals.

Reports and Analytics
